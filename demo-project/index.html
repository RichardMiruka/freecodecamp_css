Our Philosophy

Coffee is a

Behind the Cup

How we roast

01

Green Bean Intake

Raw green beans arrive and are weighed, moisture-tested, and cupped before acceptance. We reject up to 30% of lots that don't meet our benchmarks.

02

Profile Development

Our roastmaster develops a custom temperature curve for each origin — adjusting charge temperature, drum speed, and airflow to highlight the bean's best qualities.

03

The Roast

Beans are roasted in small batches (max 12kg) on our Probat drum roaster. We listen for the first crack, watching temperature curves in real time on our logging software.

04

Rest & Cupping

Freshly roasted beans rest for 48–72 hours to de-gas. Then a blind cupping is held. Only approved batches are bagged and sold — the rest are re-roasted or donated.
